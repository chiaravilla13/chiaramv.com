Cookie Policy

This page describes the use of cookies and similar technologies on the website chiaramv.com.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that visited websites send to the user’s device, where they are stored and then sent back to the same websites on subsequent visits.

Types of cookies used

Technical cookies

These are necessary for the correct functioning of the website and to allow normal browsing and use of its contents.

Analytics cookies

The website may use analytics cookies to collect aggregated information about how the site is used, such as visited pages, time spent and general interactions, with the purpose of improving content and performance.

Consent management

On the first visit, a banner allows users to accept or reject non-essential cookies. Preferences can be updated by deleting cookies from the browser and visiting the site again.

How to disable cookies

Users can manage or disable cookies directly through their browser settings. Disabling technical cookies may affect some of the website’s functions.

Third-party cookies

Some external services that may be integrated into the website can install their own cookies. For more information, users should consult the relevant privacy and cookie policies of those services.
