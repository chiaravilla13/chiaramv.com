Privacy Policy

This page explains how personal data may be collected, used and protected when you browse this website or get in touch through the available contact channels.

1. Data controller

The data controller is Chiara M. Villa.

For privacy-related requests, you can write to chiara@chiaramv.com.

2. Types of data that may be collected

While browsing this website or contacting me, the following types of personal data may be processed:

  • navigation and technical data collected through the website;
  • contact details and information voluntarily shared by email;
  • usage data collected, where enabled, through analytics tools;
  • any additional information you choose to provide when requesting an audit or other services.

3. Purposes of processing

Personal data may be processed for the following purposes:

  • to allow the website to function properly;
  • to reply to requests sent through email or other contact channels;
  • to assess whether and how a collaboration, audit or strategic path may be relevant;
  • to improve the website, content and user experience;
  • to comply with legal or regulatory obligations, where applicable.

4. Legal basis for processing

Depending on the case, personal data may be processed on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • your consent, where required;
  • the performance of pre-contractual measures requested by you;
  • compliance with legal obligations;
  • the legitimate interest in ensuring website security, functionality and service organisation.

5. Methods of processing

Personal data is processed using appropriate organisational and technical measures designed to ensure security, confidentiality and protection against unauthorised access, loss, disclosure or improper use.

Processing may take place in digital form and, where necessary, through tools used to manage communication, analytics or operational workflows.

6. Data retention

Personal data is kept for no longer than necessary in relation to the purposes for which it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required by law or justified by legitimate organisational needs.

Data shared through direct contact may be retained for the time needed to manage the request and any related follow-up.

7. Recipients and third-party services

Personal data may be processed through services used for website hosting, analytics, email management or operational support.

Where third-party providers are involved, data is handled within the limits necessary for the performance of their functions and according to their respective privacy policies and applicable law.

8. Data subject rights

Under applicable data protection law, you may have the right to:

  • access your personal data;
  • request rectification or update of inaccurate data;
  • request erasure of data, where applicable;
  • request restriction of processing;
  • object to processing, where allowed by law;
  • withdraw consent, where processing is based on consent;
  • lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority.

To exercise your rights, you can write to chiara@chiaramv.com.

9. Cookies and tracking tools

This website may use technical cookies and, where enabled, analytics tools. For more detailed information, please refer to the Cookie Policy.

10. Updates to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated over time to reflect legal, technical or organisational changes.

You are encouraged to review this page periodically.
