Cookie Policy

This page explains which cookies may be used on this website, what they are for and how you can manage your preferences.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that websites may store on your device while you browse. They can be used for technical functions, analytics, preference storage and, in some cases, marketing purposes.

This website uses cookies in a limited and functional way, with the aim of ensuring proper operation and, where enabled, improving the browsing experience.

Types of cookies that may be used

Technical cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and to provide essential features, such as navigation, session handling and saving basic preferences.

Analytics cookies

These cookies help understand, in aggregate form, how the website is used, so that content, structure and user experience can be improved.

Third-party cookies

Some features or embedded tools may involve cookies managed by third-party services. Their use depends on the settings and policies of those providers.

How consent works

When required, non-essential cookies are used only after you have expressed your choice through the cookie banner. You can accept, reject or manage your preferences according to the options made available on the site.

Technical cookies, when present, do not normally require prior consent because they are necessary for the operation of the website.

How to manage or disable cookies

From the banner

You can manage your preferences directly through the cookie banner shown on the website, where available.

From your browser

You can also limit, block or delete cookies through your browser settings. The exact steps vary depending on the browser you use.

Please note

Disabling some cookies may affect the proper functioning of certain parts of the website.

Third-party services

This website may use third-party tools for analytics, performance measurement or embedded content. Where these tools place cookies, their providers act according to their own privacy and cookie policies.

For more detailed information, you may also refer to the documentation made available by the relevant third-party services.

Updates to this policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated over time, for example to reflect legal changes, technical updates or changes in the tools used on the website.

You are encouraged to review this page periodically.

Contact
